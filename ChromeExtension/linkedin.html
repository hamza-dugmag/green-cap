      About

      I am an aspiring software developer who enjoys solving problems in creative ways: whether it be automating something I have to do often or pondering about how existing technologies can help solve real world problems. I have strong technical skills and an academic background in computer science.


      My passion lies in full stack development, AI and using my skills to create things front to back. I also enjoy working with teams of like-minded individuals to turn big ideas into a reality.
